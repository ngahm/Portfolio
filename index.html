My Story

After devoting 7 years to the transportation industry, the robust growth of the tech industry was just too hard to ignore. My interest in coding stems from my drive to create over maintain and the unique ability a technical skill brings to opening doors in an otherwise competitive market. With my current broad experience, I believe a focused trade compliments my current development as a professional and will put me in position to be a greater contributor going forward. The other attraction I have to the tech industry is the strong push for networking. It is refreshing to see industry professionals with similar interests and a willingness to grow their level of knowledge.
